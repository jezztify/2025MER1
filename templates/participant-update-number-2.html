Dear Beloved Couples,

We’re so excited to welcome you soon! Here are a few quick reminders to help you prepare for a smooth and joyful retreat experience:

What to Bring
  • Water bottle (tap water available only)
  • Toiletries, towels, and linens (or request a set for $12)
  • Warm clothes (it’s winter!) and white attire for Mass
  • Medications and personal snacks
  • Baby food, supplies, and entertainment for little ones
  • Books, toys, or gadgets for children
Social Night: Cruise Theme!

Please pack semi-formal attire and feel free to add a nautical touch. Get ready for karaoke, couple games, and lots of fun!

Venue Tips
  • The venue is remote—bring all essentials (no nearby shops/pharmacies)
  • Help us keep the venue tidy: please segregate rubbish and enjoy snacks outside rooms
  • Explore the venue and check amenities and emergency plans when you arrive
Gentle Weather Reminder

While Bella Rakha provides heaters in each room, we care about your comfort and well-being! The venue can get quite chilly in winter, so we warmly encourage you to bring plenty of cozy clothes and winter linens to help you stay comfortable throughout the retreat.

We can’t wait to set sail with you on this journey of love and faith!
