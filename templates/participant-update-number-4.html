📚 Today's Focus: Active Engagement & Humble Hearts 📚

Building on the prayerful foundation we discussed, today we'll explore how to engage fully with the retreat content and maintain a heart posture that welcomes growth and learning.

Being spiritually ready means preparing not just our minds, but our hearts to receive God's wisdom. When we combine this spiritual preparation with active engagement and humble hearts, we create the perfect environment for transformation in our marriages.

The following three attitudes will help you maximize every moment of your retreat experience.

👂 Listen Attentively & Take Notes

The seven talks contain profound wisdom for your marriage. Active listening and note-taking will help you capture insights that could transform your relationship for years to come.

1
Listen Beyond Words 🎯

Pay attention to the emotions, experiences, and heart behind each speaker's message. Sometimes the most powerful insights come from what's felt, not just heard. This same principle applies during your couple discussions - listen to your spouse's heart, not just their words, to understand their deeper feelings and needs.

2
Note Questions & Reflections 💭

Write down questions that arise and how each talk relates to your specific marriage situation. These notes will be valuable during couple discussions.

💖 Be Open & Sincere

Authenticity creates the foundation for real growth. Being open and sincere with your spouse during discussions allows God's grace to work powerfully in your marriage.

1
Share Your True Feelings 💗

Express what you're really thinking and feeling, not just what you think your spouse wants to hear. Honest sharing builds deeper intimacy.

2
Admit Areas for Growth 🌱

Be willing to acknowledge areas where your marriage needs improvement. Vulnerability opens the door to healing and transformation.

3
Receive Without Defensiveness 🛡️

When your spouse shares openly, receive their words with love and understanding, without becoming defensive or judgmental.

🤝 Be Humble & Helpful

Humility and helpfulness during your couple discussions create a safe space for honest communication and mutual growth. These attitudes foster deeper understanding and strengthen your bond.

1
Listen to Learn, Not to Win 🎓

Approach discussions with your spouse as opportunities to understand their perspective better, rather than trying to prove your point or be right.

2
Ask Clarifying Questions 🤗

When your spouse shares something, ask gentle questions to better understand their feelings and thoughts. Show genuine interest in their inner world.

3
Support Their Growth Journey 🙋

When your spouse identifies areas for improvement, offer encouragement and support rather than criticism or judgment. Be their biggest cheerleader.

4
Admit Your Own Need for Growth 🤲

Acknowledge that you too have areas to improve in your marriage. Sharing your own vulnerabilities creates mutual understanding and deeper intimacy.

"Let the wise listen and add to their learning, and let the discerning get guidance."

Remember, this retreat is not just about receiving information, but about transformation. Come with hearts ready to listen, learn, and grow together.
